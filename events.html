Events

Join us for a series of exciting events designed to inspire, educate, and connect aspiring developers and tech professionals. At HyperionDev, we host hands-on workshops, expert-led seminars, and networking sessions that keep you at the forefront of the industry. Whether you're a beginner looking to sharpen your coding skills or an experienced developer eager to explore the latest technologies, our events provide valuable insights and real-world learning opportunities.

Mastering Cybersecurity with Annah Adams

Tuesday, September 26th at 7:00 pm

Join us for an exclusive Cybersecurity Tech Talk featuring Annah Adams, a leading expert in ethical hacking and digital security. This session will cover the latest threats, best security practices, and real-world case studies to help you stay ahead in the ever-evolving cybersecurity landscape. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, this event is a must-attend for anyone looking to strengthen their cybersecurity knowledge.

AI & Machine Learning Bootcamp – Level Up Your AI Skills!

Friday, November 15th at 6:30 pm

Step into the world of Artificial Intelligence with our hands-on AI & Machine Learning Bootcamp! Led by Peter Thornhill, an AI specialist, this workshop will cover neural networks, data science fundamentals, and real-world AI applications. Whether you're new to AI or looking to sharpen your skills, this event will equip you with the tools to build your own intelligent systems.

Game Development Masterclass – Build & Launch Your First Game

Friday, October 17th at 6:30 pm

Calling all aspiring game developers! Join us for an exciting Game Development Masterclass with Bongani Mthetwa, a seasoned game designer. Learn how to design, develop, and launch your own game using industry-leading tools like Unity and Unreal Engine. Whether you dream of indie game development or working in a top gaming studio, this event will set you on the right path.

Tech Career Fair – Connect, Learn & Get Hired!

Wednesday, July 30th at 6:30 pm

Ready to kickstart your tech career? Join us at our Tech Career Fair, where top recruiters, hiring managers, and tech professionals will share insights on landing your dream job. Get resume tips, interview advice, and networking opportunities with companies looking for fresh talent. Whether you're a student, graduate, or career changer, this event is your gateway to the tech industry.
